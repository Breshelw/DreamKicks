3.) Which forms of payment can I use?

We accept the following: • All Visa and MasterCard logo debit cards • Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover credit cards • PayPal, Afterpay and Affirm- Buy Now, Pay Later

4.) How do you handle missing packages?

Dream Kick is not responsible for lost, missing, or stolen packages once the courier has picked up the package from our facility and is in route. Dream Kicks customer service will be happy to assist you in filling out a claim with the courier

5.) Can I have my order shipped outside of the United States?

At this time the only region we ship outside of the US is Puerto Rico. The options are International Economy (5-10 business days) or International Express (2-3 business days). Free shipping will not be eligible.
